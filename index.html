About us:

Dot Berrys is India's first augmented reality cake shop with delicious and innovative cake varieties of delight. Dot Berrys' cakes and other luscious products add a fresh taste to your celebrations. You can taste happiness through our creative and authentic preparations of sweet delicacies and delights. Dot Berrys is also specialized in making customized cakes by infusing anime characters, dolls, etc., for various celebrations by hearing out the customer's wish. We also design unique fondant cakes with enormous flavours and artistic fervour. Our cakes and pastries are rich in flavour and authentic in taste.
